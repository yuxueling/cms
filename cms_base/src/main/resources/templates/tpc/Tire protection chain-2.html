      Tire protection chain

      Why to use a Tire Protection Chain?

      Sharp, edged rock, muddy and slippery surfaces are a great hazard to tires – even when new. Equipment downtime and loss of productivity are the result of sudden tire failure.

      Application Areas of Tire Protection Chain

      Open Pit Mining
      Underground Mining &Tunnelling
      Quarrying
      Slag & Scrap Handling
      Traction

      The advantages of using tire protection chains are

      Open Pit Mining
      Reducing your operating costs
      Lowering your downtime
      Increasing your productivity
       

      Tire Protection Chains installation

      1.Let the loader raise the shovel, adjust one side of the tire and drive directly at tire protection chain then stop it when move to the 20 cm position of the tire protection chain. Make the tire half empty and the use about one meter iron wire through the circle of two sides of the front of the tire protection chain, and tighten it. Pull the iron wire by two men then hitch it into the trench.

      2. Back the loader, roll up the tire protection chains on the tire(drive the front when installing the tire back),a man should stand near both side of the tire, using a stick to pull the chain and then make the edgiest rhombuses assembly of the chain, both sides directly at the two sides tip of the tire, do not deviation, turn round a complete circle.

      3. Connected the tire protection chains from both sides with pin link, tools can be usedfor installing. Connected the two tips of chain with connecting assembly and put down the board for pressing well, pierce the screw, tighten up it .Spot welding later.

      4. Let the long lock chain throughout the ring of outside one by one and then connect with a stick of steel wire rope ,hitch one side of steel wire rope on it, raising the shovel and then pull tighten slowly, meanwhile, using a hammer to knock the ring which throughout the chain. Do this process for 3 or 4 times until tightens the tire protection chain.

      5. Insert a thin wire into cross of the chain for tighten and ring, taking down the steel wire rope,making the tip of chain back to ring and then through screw and tighten it .

      6.Aerate the tire to normal then the installing of the tire protection chain is over.

      7. Use the spot welding solders one side of screw of connecting assembly so that the screw can not fall off.
