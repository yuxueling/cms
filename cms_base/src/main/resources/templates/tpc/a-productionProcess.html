Production Process

1. Raw Material In Stock

We have enough raw material in stock every year. The material of connecting ring is 20CrMnTI, and diameter of connecting rings contain 12mm, 13mm, 14mm, 16mm, 18mm, 20mm and 22mm. The material of link is 40Cr, and diameter of steel bars for links contain 16mm, 18mm, 20mm. 22mm, 26mm, 28mm, 32mm and 46mm.

2. Forging links

We have many kinds of links, and we choose different links according to tire size and customer requirements. We have two forging lines, and we forge the links and spare parts for tire protection chain.

3. Cutting rings

We just bought one machine that it can cutting rings from steel bars.

4. Welding

We have three welding lines and we can weld the connecting rings with links together.

5. Checking

After Welding is completed, we will organize the chains to ensure that all of welds are full.

6. Heat treatment

After welding, we will do heat treatment for the whole chains in order to increase the hardness of chains.

7. Shot blasting

We will do shot blasting for the chains after heat treatment in order to remove burrs and oxidation skin.

8. Coating

All of chains will be coated with black paint and drying before shipment.
