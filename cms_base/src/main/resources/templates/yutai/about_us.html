    About YuTai Glass

    Shanxi yutai glass technology co., LTD. Is located in no.8 road, huitong industrial park, jinzhong development zone, shanxi demonstration zone, jinzhong city, Shanxi Province. Known as the "provincial gateway", yuci is an important hub of shanxi's communications and transportation. It is 10 kilometers away from taiyuan airport and 6 kilometers away from taiyuan expressway. The company covers an area of 35351 square meters, with a total construction area of 26000 square meters, with a total investment of 70 million yuan, is a collection of scientific research design development and production of service is a body comprehensive enterprise, is currently China's most influential glass processing base in north China, one of the company's core competitiveness from has an aggressive, innovative, with rich professional experience of the management team and technical backbone team. The company takes the perfect organization and quality system as the foundation, the standard management system as the means, takes the high-end product route and the high-quality product consciousness as the management idea, unceasingly improves the operation mechanism, strictly controls the operation risk, has laid the solid foundation for the company to continue the rapid development. Company introduces the world first-class automated production line, with years of experience in production technology and services, according to the different requirements of customers processing energy-saving safety glass, low-e coated glass (low-e) hollow glass, safety of toughened glass, laminated glass, fire-resistant glass, bullet-proof glass, coated glass, optical glass, etc. Series of products, electric shutter, building energy saving glass is the high-end products. Mainly used in the high-end building glass exterior wall, interior decoration, as well as residential doors and Windows.

    The energy-efficient insulating glass developed Shanxi Yutai Glass Technology Co., Ltd., in accordance with the National Civil Building Energy Conservation Regulations, is in compliance with the requirements of building energy-saving product standards, and is listed in the province's building energy-saving products (technology) promotion catalogue, and won the "Shanxi" The Provincial Building Energy-Saving Products (Technology) Promotion Certificate is used in civil construction projects throughout the province. Based on the principle of good faith management, the company has provided high-quality products for customers. It has won various honorary certificates issued by the government and won the “Technology Progress Promotion Award” for the Chinese building door and window curtain wall industry.
