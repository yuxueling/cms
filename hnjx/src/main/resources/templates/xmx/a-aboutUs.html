About A & M Manufacturing Co.Ltd



Based in the UK, A&M Manufacturing Co.Ltd has over 30 year experience in the foundry and general metal working industry. With over 10 years experience in building close relations with Taiwan and China we have a large manufacturing network base enabling us to be extremely competitive and at the same time produce parts to the highest quality.

We specialize in a wide range of processes including ALL types of casting, fabrications, forgings, stampings and machined from solid parts. We can supply parts in small, medium and large volume, from class 1 aerospace castings to small machined turned parts. At design stage we can advise on both shape and material specification.

Parts are supplied finished as to drawing requirements ie; including ALL finishing processes.



Metal products supplied



Investment Casting, Gravity and Pressure Die Castings, Fabrications, Forgings, Stampings, Lazer and Flame Cutting, MIM (Metal Injection Moulding), Extrusions

Machining / Finishing



Apart from supplying all the above parts fully machined we also machine components from bar stock including CNC Milling, Turning, Grinding, Lapping, Broaching etc.

All finishing processes are carried out as to your requirements including: Heat treatment, Painting / Powder Coating, Plating, Anodising, Phosphate and Hard Facing, Chemi-Black.
